## üìù Best Practices

### **LinkedIn Ads:**
1. **A/B Test Everything**
   - Headlines
   - Images/videos
   - CTAs
   - Targeting

2. **Use LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms**
   - Pre-fills data from LinkedIn profile
   - Higher conversion rates
   - Lower cost per lead

3. **Video Content Performs Best**
   - 3x higher engagement
   - Better conversion rates
   - More memorable

4. **Time Your Ads**
   - Tuesday-Thursday: Best performance
   - 8-10 AM and 12-1 PM: Peak engagement
   - Avoid weekends

### **Landing Page:**
1. **Single Focus**
   - One clear CTA
   - Remove navigation distractions
   - Focus on conversion

2. **Speed Matters**
   - < 3 second load time
   - Optimize images
   - Minimize JavaScript

3. **Trust Signals**
   - Client logos
   - Testimonials
   - Security badges
   - Money-back guarantee (if applicable)
